Meet the Characters

BIB

The friendly robot in search of nature, and himself. He’s conflicted and he’s friendly, but can he also be fierce?

THE HAWKS

Diabolical or just doing their job ruling the forest? You decide. These hawks have got a bone to pick with anyone standing in their way.

THE KITTEN

Shrouded in mystery and cuteness, this kitten has some tricks up her paw. You’ll have to play if you want to find out the fate of this fuzzy friend...or foe.
